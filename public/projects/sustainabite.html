Sustainabite

April 2025 Applied Machine Learning Hackathon Project

Sustainabite is a smart, student-focused platform designed to reduce food waste by helping users turn what they already have into delicious meals. By simply uploading a photo of pantry items or a fridge snapshot, users receive curated recipe recommendations based on their available ingredients, preferred cuisine, and time constraints. At its core, Sustainabite combines image recognition and machine learning to bridge the gap between what’s in your kitchen and what ends up on your plate.

Technical Overview

The platform uses OpenAI’s CLIP model to detect ingredients directly from uploaded images, which are matched against a massive recipe dataset of over 500,000. A fine-tuned retrieval system filters recipes based on real-time inputs. Two additional ML models predict cuisine and cooking time. Built with Streamlit, the app emphasizes usability, accessibility, and fast feedback loops.
