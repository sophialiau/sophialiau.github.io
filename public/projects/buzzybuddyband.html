Buzzy Buddy Band

January - April 2025 Engineering Design & Development BMEG 257 Project

The Buzzy Buddy Band is a wearable device designed to assist diabetic children with medication adherence. It utilizes a combination of sensors and haptic feedback to alert users to actionable changes in blood glucose, all in a child-focused watch desgin. This project was developed as part of my BMEG 257 course at the University of British Columbia, where I collaborated with a team to create an innovative solution that enhances accessibility and safety for our target users and their families.
