Bike Lane Proposal

October 2023 Engineering Design & Development APSC 100 Project

The bike lane proposal is a comprehensive design project aimed at enhancing urban mobility and safety in Vancouver. It focuses on the development of a dedicated bike lane along a major arterial road, addressing the growing need for sustainable transportation options. The project includes detailed stakeholder specifications, weighted decision matrices, and strategies to ensure the successful implementation of the bike lane. This project was part of my APSC 100 course at the University of British Columbia, where I collaborated with a team to create a proposal that balances technical feasibility with community needs.
